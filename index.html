Examples

Deadline Email

Subject Options

  • "We're short — and the clock is brutal."
  • "Midnight hits whether we're ready or not."

Look, I'm not going to sugarcoat this. We're $2,400 short of our FEC deadline goal — and that number is going to be public. I need 47 more donors by midnight…

Goal: drive same-day donations

Match Day Email

Subject Options

  • "Your $25 just became $50."
  • "A donor is matching everything until midnight."

A generous supporter is matching every single dollar you give today — up to $25,000 total. That means your $25 turns into $50. Your $100 turns into $200…

Goal: increase average gift with match framing

Event Follow-Up

Subject Options

  • "You showed up. That matters."
  • "After last night, I had to write you."

I saw you at the rally last night. The energy was incredible. But energy doesn't pay for TV ads — and we need $15,000 by Friday to lock in our next buy…

Goal: convert warm supporters into donors

FAQ

Common Questions

How fast can you start?
Same day in most cases. Once you subscribe, you can submit your first request immediately.
What does "one request at a time" mean?
You can submit unlimited requests — we work through them one at a time so nothing gets rushed. As soon as one is approved, the next goes in the queue.
Will the emails sound like us?
Yes. We start by reviewing 3–5 past emails, key talking points, and voice notes. You'll also get revisions until it's right.
Do you send the emails for us?
We deliver ready-to-send copy you can paste into your platform. If you want hands-on sending, we can discuss add-ons.
What platforms do you work with?
ActBlue/NGP workflows plus common email tools like Mailchimp, Action Network, HubSpot, and more. If you can send emails, we can write for it.
Is there a contract?
No long-term contract. Month-to-month. Pause or cancel anytime.
