FAQ

Common Questions

How fast can you start writing our emails?
Fast. After a 30-minute onboarding call, we deliver your first email draft within 48 hours. Most clients are fully ramped and sending within one week.
What does the 10% commission cover?
The commission is based on total dollars raised through the emails we write and send. It aligns our incentives — we only win when you raise more. The flat retainer covers our writing, strategy, and testing work.
Will the emails sound like our candidate/org?
Yes. We do a thorough voice-match during onboarding — reviewing past communications, speeches, and social content. You approve every email before it sends, so nothing goes out that doesn't feel like you.
What email platforms do you work with?
We integrate with all major platforms — NationBuilder, NGP VAN, Action Network, Mailchimp, Constant Contact, ActiveCampaign, and more. We'll work with whatever your campaign already uses.
Is there a long-term contract?
No. All plans are month-to-month. Cancel anytime with no penalty. We believe our work speaks for itself — you stay because the results are there, not because of a contract.
