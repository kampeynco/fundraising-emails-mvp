Pricing Questions

What does "unlimited revisions" mean exactly?
Unlimited revisions during the scheduled window for that email. Once a draft is delivered, you can request as many changes as needed before it's finalized. This doesn't cover reopening drafts weeks later.
Can I change my plan later?
Yes — upgrade or downgrade anytime. Changes take effect on your next billing cycle. You can also pause if you're between campaigns.
How does Rapid Response work?
Rapid Response is a per-email add-on for urgent moments. Submit your request and get a complete draft within 24 business hours. Subject lines, preview text, and revisions are all included. Available on any plan.
Is there a contract?
No long-term contract. Month-to-month. Pause or cancel anytime — and keep everything we've written.
