Pricing Questions

What does "unlimited revisions" mean exactly?
Unlimited revisions during the scheduled window for that email. Once a draft is delivered, you can request as many changes as needed before it's finalized.
Can I change my plan later?
Yes — upgrade or downgrade anytime. Changes take effect on your next billing cycle. You can also pause if you're between campaigns.
How does Rapid Response work?
Rapid Response is a monthly add-on ($995/mo) for campaigns sending 3+ emails/week. It gives you 24-hour turnaround on urgent emails — breaking news, opposition hits, deadline surprises. Subject lines, preview text, and revisions are all included.
Is there a contract?
No long-term contract. Month-to-month. Pause or cancel anytime — and keep everything we've written.
